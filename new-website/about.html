Our Story

The initial idea for SnowShoe was to provide an experiential access to data. Although the technology has developed far beyond our original concept, this simple idea encouraged a personal interaction, which is incredibly relevant to what we offer today.

Bringing people, brands and experiences together in an engaging way is what made SnowShoe unique 7 years ago and what attracts organizations to our technology today. By eliminating equipment and development costs, and the potential fraud other options come with, our mobile authentication platform encourages the kind of experience consumers are looking for with the brands they choose.

And the SnowShoe Stamp technology only gets better as our dedicated team focuses on developing high-value improvements, collaboration with customers to conceptualize new applications, and revealing program and customer insights that drive business decisions.

We measure success for SnowShow in our customers’ success. We celebrate when SnowShoe Stamp technology is applied to real-world problems that build our customers’ brand loyalty and impacts revenue.

Our team of engineering, product and customer success professionals have worked together long enough to call ourselves family. The kind of family you choose to spend time with. And trust to always do the right thing.
